Biography

My name is François Beaubert, I’m an Assistant Professor in Fluid Mechanics at INSA Hauts de France and LAMIH lab.

  • My research interests include Turbulence Modelization, Turbulence Control, Swirling Flows, Heat and Mass Transfer.
  • I teach Fluid Mechanics, Numerical Methods in Fluid Mechanics, Turbulence Modelization and Aeroacoustics.
